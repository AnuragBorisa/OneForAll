# Constitution

## Mission

Create the best personal AI intelligence feed for staying current on AI news, workflows, launches, and practical usage.

## Product Standards

### 1. Practicality First

Every major feature should improve a user's ability to understand or apply AI developments, not just consume more content.

### 2. Source Transparency

Every surfaced item must retain clear provenance: source platform, author, URL, and timing.

### 3. Cross-Source Thinking

The system should merge overlapping stories and compare perspectives across platforms where feasible.

### 4. Clarity Over Hype

Generated explanations should reduce confusion, identify uncertainty, and avoid exaggerated claims.

### 5. Progressive Delivery

The system should be built in thin vertical slices that can be tested with real content early.

## Technical Standards

- Prefer simple reliable pipelines over premature autonomy.
- Treat ingestion, normalization, and summarization as separate stages.
- Design for reprocessing as prompts and models improve.
- Maintain explicit schemas for raw content, normalized content, and generated explanations.

## Product Surface Standards

- Primary surface: web app
- Secondary surfaces: digest channels such as WhatsApp
- Interfaces should optimize scanability, filtering, and revisitation
