# Product Reference

## Users

- AI-curious operators who want fast understanding without chasing many feeds
- Builders who want real workflows and practical implementation ideas
- Professionals who need daily AI updates filtered by usefulness

## Main Job

Help users understand new AI developments and decide whether to act on them.

## MVP Surface

- Personalized web feed
- Item detail with practical explanation
- Save and revisit
- Topic filters
- Daily digest

## Non-Goals For MVP

- Full social posting platform
- Community comments
- Complex cross-user recommendation network
- Native mobile apps
